Let's Identify Some Hazards

Overloaded Socket!

Overloading the socket can cause the socket to overheat and cause a fire!

Build up of combustible!

Stacks of easily flammable items such as Newspapers, paper boxes etc. can be a potential source for fire!

Congested Corridor!

The corridor is your passageway to safety during emergencies. Do not store items that would obstruct your escape!

Frayed Cables!

Frayed cables cause the live wires to be exposed and can be vulnerable to electric fires. Do check and replace them regularly!

Non-Illuminated Exit Signs!

Exit signs are there to guide us to safety in emergencies. Always ensure that they are illuminated.

Overturn your pails!

Pails are the main source stagnant water and Aedes Mosquito can breed in them. Overturn the pails to protect your family from Dengue!

Clear the water in the plant plates!

Plant plates are commonly neglected as a breeding spot for Aedes Mosquito. Check and clear them now to protect your family from Dengue!

Is your fire extinguisher well maintained?

You wouldn't want your fire extinguisher to fail in a real emergency. Check the label to see if it is serviceable.
